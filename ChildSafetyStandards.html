Child Safety Standards & CSAE Policy Nooria is committed to providing a safe environment for all users. We have a zero-tolerance policy regarding Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE). 1. Prevention and Detection We implement proactive measures to prevent the upload and sharing of prohibited content. Any content identified as CSAM is immediately removed, and the associated account is permanently banned. 2. Reporting Mechanism Nooria allows users to report any child safety concerns directly within the app. Every report is reviewed by our safety team with high priority. 3. Compliance and Law Enforcement We comply with all relevant child safety laws. In accordance with legal requirements, we report any incidents of CSAM/CSAE to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and other relevant regional authorities. 4.
